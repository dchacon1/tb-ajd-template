AJD Career Path examples

These examples are to showcase how vertile the product can be and give developers and creative alike examples they can follow. Please note that it is not a requirment that the job titles be linkabe.

Example 1

Simple 1 line path

  • Digital Strategist
  • Client Strategist
  • Regional Vice President

Example 2

One job splits to two paths

  • Pet Stylist
  • Salon Leaders
  • Store Department Manager
    District Academy Trainer
  • Assistant Store Leader
    Quality Education Manager
  • Store Leader
    Home Office Salon Team

Example 3

One job splits to multiple paths and then converges again

  • Digital Strategist
  • Client Strategist 1
    Client Strategist 2
    Client Strategist 3
  • VP Client Strategist 2
    VP Client Strategist 3
  • Regional Vice President

Example 4

Two jobs converge into one path and then back and forth

  • Route Sales Representative
    Warehouse Associate
  • Depot Training Specialist
    Service Ops Specialist
  • Area Manager
  • Zone Logistics Mgr
  • Zone Manager
  • Division Operations / Logistics
    National Operations / Logistics
    • Division Vice President

Example 5

One job splits into two paths and one of those jobs splits into another two paths and all converge

  • View Junior Developer leads to UI Team Lead, Maintenance Team Lead, or Product Developer.
  • View Senior Developer leads to UI Manager.
    View Product Developer leads to Product Manager.
  • View UI Manager leads to Digital Director.
    View Product Manager leads to Product Director.
  • View Maintenance Team Lead leads to UI Manager.
    View Digital Assistant Director leads to Vice President of Software.
  • View Digital Director leads to Vice President of Software.
    View Product Director leads to Vice President of Software.
  • View Vice President leads to Senior Developer.
